About Pulse Grove

Pulse Grove is an original Flutter game where you catch falling light, protect focus, and grow a luminous grove across short reflex runs.

Features

  • Classic and Zen game modes.
  • Consumable coin packs through Google Play Billing.
  • Coin-spending tools and unlockable visual themes.
  • Local profile, stats, and transaction history.
  • English, Vietnamese, Chinese, Thai, Hindi, and Russian app languages.
