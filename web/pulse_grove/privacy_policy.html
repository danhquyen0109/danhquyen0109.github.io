Privacy Policy

App: Pulse Grove

Contact: phamdanhquyen@gmail.com

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Pulse Grove is an Android-first Flutter game. We respect your privacy and collect only the data needed to run optional game features.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app stores gameplay data locally, including coins, selected language, player name, theme ownership, tool inventory, stats, and purchase history. This information is saved on your device using local storage.

In-App Purchases

Pulse Grove offers optional consumable coin packs through Google Play Billing. Google processes payment information. The app receives purchase status and product identifiers so coins can be credited and purchase history can be shown.

Purchase Logging

After a purchase is fulfilled, the app may send a small purchase log containing player name, app name, product id, purchase amount, and timestamp to a private reporting endpoint. This helps us verify sales and support users. Payment card details are never sent to us.

Player Name

You may enter or edit a player name in Profile. On Android, the app may try to prefill this from another local game in the same app family when available. You can always change it manually.

Children

Pulse Grove is a general audience game and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us for removal.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. The latest version will be posted on this page.
