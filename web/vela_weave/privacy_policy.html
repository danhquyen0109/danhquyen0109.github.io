Overview

Vela Weave is an Android-first Flutter game. This policy explains the limited information used for gameplay, optional purchases, purchase support, and app updates.

Information Stored on Your Device

The app stores player name, language, audio and haptic preferences, coins, owned tools and palettes, mission progress, scores, statistics, processed purchase tokens, and local transaction history. This data supports game continuity and is normally kept on your device until you clear app data or uninstall the app.

Optional In-App Purchases

Consumable coin packs are processed by Google Play Billing. Google handles your account and payment details. Vela Weave receives product identifiers, price information, purchase status, and a purchase token so it can credit coins, prevent duplicate fulfillment, and consume the product for repeat purchase. We do not receive payment card numbers.

Purchase Logs

After coins are credited and local history is saved, the app may send a purchase record to a private reporting endpoint. The record can include the saved player name, app name, product ID, purchase amount, timestamp, week number, and year. It is used for purchase verification, accounting, and support. A logging failure does not block your in-game benefit.

Player Name

You may enter, edit, or leave the player name blank. On Android, Vela Weave may attempt to prefill a locally saved name from Treasure Quest when platform access permits. Manual entry is always available in Profile.

Updates and External Pages

On Android, the app can ask Google Play whether an update is available. Legal and information links may open this website in an in-app web view or your browser. Those services may process standard network information under their own policies.

Advertising, Subscriptions, and Sharing

Vela Weave contains no advertising and no subscriptions. We do not sell personal information. Purchase records are used only for operating and supporting the app.

Children

Vela Weave is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly request personal information from children. A parent or guardian may contact us to request review or deletion of a purchase-support record.

Security and Retention

We use reasonable safeguards for the limited records described above. No transmission or storage method is completely secure. Local data remains until removed from the device; purchase-support records may be retained as needed for accounting, fraud prevention, and user support.

Your Choices

You can edit the player name, avoid optional purchases, reset gameplay progress, clear application data, or uninstall Vela Weave. For a question or deletion request concerning a purchase-support record, email the contact address above with enough purchase detail for us to locate it.

Changes

We may revise this policy when the app or legal requirements change. The latest version and update date will remain on this page.
