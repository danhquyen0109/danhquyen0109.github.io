Vela Weave

Vela Weave is an original Flutter timing game. Match three moving light threads at the stitch line, build precise combos, complete 12 patterns, and shape a personal workshop of useful tools and color palettes.

Inside the Game

  • Three-lane timing gameplay with Perfect and Good stitch grades.
  • 12 missions with rising pace, stars, rewards, and persistent progress.
  • Guard, Calm, and Echo spools that change a live run.
  • Unlockable thread palettes, local profile, stats, and transaction history.
  • English and Vietnamese interface languages.
  • Optional consumable coin packs through Google Play on Android.

Original Work

The gameplay concept, interface, logo, and visual assets were created specifically for Vela Weave. The project does not use artwork or ideas copied from another game.
