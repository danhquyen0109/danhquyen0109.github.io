Privacy Policy for Lumen Reef

Effective date: July 3, 2026

Lumen Reef is a Flutter puzzle game published for Android by Galax Team. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

Information We Store on Your Device

The app stores gameplay data locally, including coins, tools, themes, language preference, player name, statistics, and purchase history. This data is used to keep your game progress and preferences.

Player Name Prefill

On Android, Lumen Reef may attempt to read the saved player name from the app Treasure Quest if it is installed on the same device. This is used only to prefill your profile. You can edit or ignore the name at any time. If the read is unavailable, the app falls back to manual entry.

Purchases

Lumen Reef uses Google Play Billing for optional consumable coin packs. Purchase processing is handled by Google Play. After a successful purchase is fulfilled in the app, a purchase log may be sent to our purchase log endpoint with player name, app name, product ID, amount, timestamp, week, and year. This log helps reconcile purchases and support players. Failed logging does not affect purchase fulfillment.

Third-Party Services

The app may use Google Play services for billing and in-app updates. Google Play handles data according to Google's own privacy policies.

Children

Lumen Reef is a casual puzzle game and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us so we can help remove it where possible.
