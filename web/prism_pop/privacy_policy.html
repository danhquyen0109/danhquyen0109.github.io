Privacy Policy for Prism Pop

Effective date: July 3, 2026

Prism Pop is a Flutter puzzle game for Android. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information Stored on Your Device

Prism Pop stores gameplay data locally, including coins, boosters, themes, language preference, player name, statistics, and purchase history. This data is used to keep your progress and preferences available on the device.

Player Name Prefill

On Android, Prism Pop may try to read the saved player name from Treasure Quest if that app is installed on the same device. This is used only to prefill your Prism Pop profile. You can edit the name manually at any time. If the read is unavailable, the app falls back to manual entry.

Purchases

Prism Pop uses Google Play Billing for optional consumable coin packs. Purchase processing is handled by Google Play. After a purchase is successfully fulfilled in the app, Prism Pop may send a purchase log containing player name, app name, product ID, amount, timestamp, week, and year to help reconcile purchases and support players. A logging failure does not block purchase fulfillment.

Third-Party Services

The app may use Google Play services for billing and in-app updates. Google Play handles data according to Google's own privacy policies.

Children

Prism Pop does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us so we can help remove it where possible.

Data Deletion Requests

You can request deletion of purchase log data associated with your player name by emailing danhquyen.work@gmail.com. Include the app name, your player name, and any relevant purchase details. Local game progress and settings can be deleted by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage on your device.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact danhquyen.work@gmail.com.
